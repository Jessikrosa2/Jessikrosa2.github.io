Hot Harissa Pita Chips

These harissa-spiced pita chips are a bold, fiery twist on the iconic Cava chips, made at home and even better. with crispy edges and a tender center, then coated in a smoky, tangy spice blend. They're perfect for scooping up your favorite dip or serving alongside a Mediterranean dinner bowl.


Recipe Facts

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 20 minutes
  • Yields: 4

Ingredients

  • pita breads, cut into triangles
  • vegetable oil for frying

Spice Blend

  • 1 tablespoon sweet paprika
  • 1 tablespoon sun-dried tomatoe powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayanne powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon lemon pepper

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the sun-dried tomatoe powder, sweet paprika, cayanne, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and lemon pepper. Set aside.
  2. Cut each pita into triangles - about 8 pieces per pita.
  3. Pour vegetable oil into a skillet or frying pan and heat it to around 325-350 f.
  4. Fry the pita triangles in batches, without overcrowding the pan, until the edges are golden and crispy and the centers still have a bit of softness.
  5. Transfer the chips to a wire rack or paper towel to drain excess oil.
  6. While still warm, place chips in a large bowl and sprinkle the spice mix over them. Toss to coat evenly.
  7. Serve immediately your favorite dip, or cool completely and store in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
